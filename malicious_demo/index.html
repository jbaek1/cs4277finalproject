Breaking: Local Tech Conference Returns Next Month

By Staff Reporter • April 22, 2026

The annual regional technology conference is set to return next month after a two-year hiatus, organizers announced Tuesday. The event will feature keynote speakers from several major software companies and a series of workshops on emerging topics in cybersecurity and artificial intelligence.

Tickets are expected to sell quickly. Early registration opens this Friday at 9 a.m. and attendees who register before May 1 receive a 20% discount. Full details are available on the official conference website.

"We are thrilled to bring the community back together," said conference director Alex Morales. "This year's program is our most ambitious yet."
